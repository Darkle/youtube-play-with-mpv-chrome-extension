Default Volume Level

Sets the volume that each video will be opened with.

(you can change the video volume once the video is opened)

Always On Top

Makes the mpv video stay on top of all other windows

Youtube Video Quality

Sets the video quality to get from Youtube

MPV Window Size

The size of the mpv window

Don't Let Youtube Page Video Autoload

This setting disables Youtube videos automatically loading when you are viewing a Youtube video page

Instead of the video loading, it will give you a choice of either opening the video in mpv or letting it load normally in the page
